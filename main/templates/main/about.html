About

This is a website developed for the teachers to upload their notes, and the students to download all the notes available, in a single place.
You can search for the notes by their name, author, or label.

The user account page has the history of all the notes You have downlaoded.
If you have a moderator account, You can also upload notes, alter them, or delete them.
All the notes uploaded by you are available in your user accounts page.

A moderator account is an account with special previliges to upload, edit or delete notes on this site.
If you are willing to be a volunteer who will upload the notes regularly, feel free to apply for a moderator account here.
